Pilau Recipe

Description

A simple beef/goat pilau recipe, perfect for a hearty homemade meal

Ingredients

  • Half a cup of rice
  • Beef, cut into small pieces
  • 4 onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomato paste
  • Soy Sauce
  • Pilau Masala
  • Cooking Oil
  • Water

Method/Steps

  1. Brown the Meat: Boil beef or goat until tender, then sauté it to achieve a deep brown sear.
  2. Caramelize Onions: Sauté onions in oil until they are dark golden-brown; this is the most critical step for the final color.
  3. Bloom the Spices: Add the pilau masala to the oil and onions to release the essential oils and fragrance.
  4. Simmer: Add washed rice and broth (or water), then cook on low heat using the absorption method until the rice is fluffy and the grains are separate.

Common Accompaniments

  • Kachumbari: A fresh tomato and onion salad that provides a zesty contrast to the rich spices.
  • Pilipili: A hot chili sauce or fried chili for those who prefer extra heat.
  • Banana: Often served with a side of ripe banana to balance the savory flavors with a hint of sweetness.
