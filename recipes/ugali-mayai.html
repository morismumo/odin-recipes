Ugali-Mayai

Description

Ugali is a meal made up of maize flour mixed with water, and mayai are Eggs Made with Tomatoes and onions

Ingredients

  • Maize flour
  • water
  • 4 eggs
  • two tomatoes
  • spices

Method/Steps

Lets prepare ugali

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in a dash of oil. first let’s Cook Ugali in a pan boil two cups of water
  2. When the water boils , make sure you have a wooden spoon, which you will use to stir the maize flour and water together to make ugali.
  3. Use a wooden spoon to stir the maize flour and the boiling water together. Continue adding the flour till you fill the ugali is firm.
  4. Make sure the ugali is not too sticky and not too hard while stirring. Continue to stir the Ugali till u smell an amazing aroma. You are supposed to stir the ugali for maximum 20 minutes while taking mini breaks in between.
  5. After 20 minutes ,mould the Ugali into a mountain shape like while still in the pan, then flip it onto a plate.

Lets prepare scrambled eggs

  1. Take Four eggs , mix them together in a bowl
  2. In a pan add oil , then chopped tomatoes and onions.
  3. Cook them for five minutes then add the four eggs.
  4. Add your favourite spices, salt then mix everything together till the eggs are scrambled.
  5. Your food is ready to serve
