Githeri Recipe

Description

Traditional githeri is a mix of traditional white corn and beans

Ingredients

  • yellow beans, different varietes can work too
  • traditional white corn
  • Onion, garlic, mild curry powder, ground cumin, and smoked paprika for some heat
  • Cooking Oil
  • Water

Method/Steps

  1. Saut√© onion and garlic in a dash of oil.
  2. Add tomato paste, curry powder, cumin, and smoked paprika.
  3. Next, add crushed tomatoes and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the beans, corn, and vegetable broth. Bring the liquid to a boil. Reduce to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes until the gravy has reduced and thickened.
